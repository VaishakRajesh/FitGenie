EXERCISE GUIDE - DETAILED INSTRUCTIONS FOR COMMON EXERCISES

1. SQUATS

Target: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, core.

Form:
- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly outward.
- Keep chest up, back straight, and core tight.
- Lower body as if sitting in a chair, going until thighs are parallel to ground.
- Keep knees tracking over toes (not caving inward).
- Drive through heels to return to starting position.

Common Mistakes:
- Knees caving inward (weakened hips/glutes)
- Rounding lower back (core not engaged)
- Heels lifting off ground (poor ankle mobility)
- Not going deep enough (reduce range of motion benefit)

Variations: Goblet squat, front squat, Bulgarian split squat, box squat.

2. DEADLIFTS

Target: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back, traps, forearms.

Form (Conventional):
- Stand with feet hip-width apart, barbell over mid-foot.
- Hinge at hips, bend knees slightly, grip bar just outside legs.
- Keep back straight, shoulders slightly over bar.
- Drive through heels, extending hips and knees simultaneously.
- Lock out at top with glutes squeezed, then lower with control.

Common Mistakes:
- Rounding the lower back (start with lighter weight)
- Bar drifting away from shins (keep bar close)
- Jerking the weight off floor (use controlled tension)
- Hyperextending at top (excessive back arch)

Variations: Romanian deadlift, sumo deadlift, single-leg deadlift, trap bar deadlift.

3. BENCH PRESS

Target: Pectorals, anterior deltoids, triceps.

Form:
- Lie on bench, eyes under bar, feet planted on floor.
- Retract shoulder blades (pinch them together).
- Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.
- Unrack and lower bar to mid-chest with elbows at 45-75 degrees.
- Press bar back up explosively, keeping shoulders retracted.

Common Mistakes:
- Bouncing bar off chest (loses tension, risk of injury)
- Flaring elbows (shoulder impingement risk)
- Arching back excessively (cheating range of motion)
- Uneven grip (asymmetrical development)

Variations: Incline press, decline press, dumbbell press, close-grip press.

4. PULL-UPS

Target: Latissimus dorsi, biceps, rhomboids, traps.

Form:
- Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder-width, palms facing away.
- Hang with arms fully extended.
- Pull yourself up by driving elbows down and back.
- Continue until chin is over bar.
- Lower with control to full extension.

Common Mistakes:
- Kipping/using momentum (reduces muscle activation)
- Not going to full extension (shortened range of motion)
- Chin not clearing bar (incomplete rep)
- Shrugging shoulders (traps taking over)

Variations: Chin-ups (palms facing), neutral grip, assisted pull-ups, negative pull-ups.

5. OVERHEAD PRESS

Target: Deltoids, triceps, upper chest.

Form:
- Stand with feet hip-width apart, bar at shoulder height.
- Grip bar slightly wider than shoulders, elbows slightly forward.
- Tighten core and glutes.
- Press bar directly overhead until arms are fully extended.
- Lower bar to shoulders with control.

Common Mistakes:
- Arching back excessively (use core to stabilize)
- Pressing in front of face (bar should travel straight up)
- Not locking out at top (reduces shoulder activation)
- Using legs to push (unless doing push press)
